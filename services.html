Financial Assistance to Blind ESM (Scheme)

Eligibility: Blind Ex-servicemen (100% Blind). (Must submit Life & Income Cert. every November).

Benefits Provided: Rs.3000/- per month with annual increase of Rs.400/- w.e.f. 1.11.2017.

Required: NOC from District Social Welfare Dept (not receiving other financial assist.).
